AquaHealth Africa Initiative (AHAI)

Empowering communities through clean water, public health, and environmental resilience.

AHAI is a community-driven nonprofit committed to improving access to clean water, strengthening community health systems, and promoting environmental sustainability across Africa.

Our Focus Areas

  • Clean water & sanitation (WASH)
  • Public health services
  • Environmental conservation
  • Renewable energy for community well-being
